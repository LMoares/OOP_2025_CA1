Mixed or Contaminated Solvents
- Handling:
- Store in sealed, clearly labeled containers.
- Keep away from heat, sparks, and direct sunlight.
- Never pour down drains or mix with other chemicals.
- Where to Dispose:
- Bring to Household Hazardous Waste (HHW) collection points or civic 
  amenity sites .
- Solvent recovery facilities can reclaim usable fractions.
- PPE Required:
- Nitrile gloves, chemical-resistant apron, safety goggles, 
  and good ventilation or respirator if fumes are strong.

üßª Contaminated Rags or Paper Towels
- Handling:
- Store in sealed bags or containers.
- Do not compost or recycle if contaminated with chemicals/oils.
- Where to Dispose:
- HHW facilities if soaked with solvents/oils.
- General waste if only lightly soiled with food.
- PPE Required:
- Gloves; goggles if handling large amounts soaked with chemicals.
