Toxic:
Bring to your local amenity site.
DO NOT PUT IN HOUSEHOLD BINS!
PPE:
Fluorescent Bulb (CFL or Tube)   
Recommended PPE & Key Actions:
PPE: Disposable gloves (to protect against glass cuts and residue). 
Clean-up Actions: 
1. DO NOT USE A VACUUM OR BROOM. These spread the mercury. 
2. Carefully scoop up large pieces of glass with stiff paper or cardboard. 
3. Use sticky tape (like duct tape) to pick up remaining powder 
and small fragments. 
4. Wipe the area with a damp paper towel or wet wipe. 
5. Place all debris (glass, tape, paper towels, and gloves) 
in a glass jar with a metal lid or a sturdy, sealed plastic bag, 
and take it outside immediately for hazardous disposal.
Mercury Thermometer
PPE: Disposable gloves (must be removed and sealed after cleanup). 
Clean-up Actions: 1. DO NOT USE A VACUUM OR BROOM. 
2. Use two pieces of stiff paper or cardboard to gently push the liquid 
silver beads of mercury together into a single, manageable ball. 
3. Use an eyedropper or sticky tape to pick up the mercury and 
place it onto a damp paper towel. 
4. Place the broken glass, all mercury, 
and cleanup materials (paper, tape, gloves) 
into a sealed, airtight glass container (best option) or a double-sealed, 
heavy-duty plastic bag, and label it "DANGER: Mercury Spill."
